Enroll Domestic Students

Please access and review our courses on our website or make an appointment to discuss your career options and the most suitable course for you by contacting the Student Services Manager by sending an email to info@ransford.edu.au or call our office on (02) 9689 2176 to discuss your course options. Ransford offers a diverse-range of nationally recognized courses in Business & Management, Hospitality, Health, Information Technology & Community Services all designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge required to succeed in today’s dynamic global workplace. Our Diploma and Advanced Diploma courses also could provide a pathway to a range of popular Australian universities. Once you have chosen a course, you can enroll by downloading an enrollment form from our website or request an enrollment form from the reception. You must complete the enrollment form fully and submit it to Ransford college by either emailing to info@ransford.edu.au or in person to the reception desk. Once your enrollment form is received you will be sent a Letter of Offer (Student Agreement) offering you a place in our course and you will be required to pay fees for the first term or as agreed by Ransford College. Let us know if you need to establish an arrangement for your special needs or your learning style.
